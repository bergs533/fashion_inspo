BACK IN TIME
70s
about
Romantic flowy blouses, flared pants, and high waisted anything were all the rage during this era. Velvet, satin, corduroy, and suede textures further gave detail to many pieces. Icons like David Bowie and Elton John challenged gender norms with their chosen stage outfits, while the disco scene and hippie movement had their respective spheres of influence regarding fashion. More than anything, the seventies were fun. With groovy patterns, and funky accessories from platforms to statement earrings, this time has surely gone down in fashion history.
