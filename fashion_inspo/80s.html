BACK IN TIME
80s
about
Over-sized fits and denim characterized much of the casual wear of this time. On the other hand, those who wanted to step out would sport bright colors and reach high heights with their hair, yes hair! Voluminous perms were all the rage. Other specific trends included turtlenecks, aerobic/exercise wear, and broad shouldered tops, or more notoriously known, shoulder pads. Madonna, Princess Diana, and Cher served as fashion icons and influencers.
