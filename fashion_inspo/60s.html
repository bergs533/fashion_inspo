BACK IN TIME
60s
about
With emerging movements towards female sexual freedom in the sixties, came less modest styles. For instance, this decade birthed and popularized the mini-skirt and mini-dress, both of which had shorter and thus more 'risque' hemlines than usual. Other notable trends were bob hairstyles, plaid, shift/shapeless dresses, and turtlenecks. Some inspirations for many women in this time were first lady Jackie Kennedy's classy, tailored style and the 'mod' subculture's style, characterized by bolder colors and more androgynous silhouettes than the world was used to.
