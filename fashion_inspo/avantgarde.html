about
Avant Garde fashion is characterized by the use of eccentric, unorthodox, and experimental design choices. Often, pieces in this sector of fashion resemble sculptural artworks more than articles of clothing. Avant Garde peices seldom look practical, or even appealing, but they certainly always turn heads and cause us to rethink the bounds by which we define fashion.
