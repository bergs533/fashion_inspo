her style
Rogers commonly takes on a retro look, employing bold primary colors, unorthodox silhouettes, and lots of elements that create movement while she is on stage, from fringe to capes (yes, capes!) Her style communicates youth, creativity, and fun. It reflects her spunky, yet down-to-earth and authentic personality - that she is trying to impress no one nor be anyone, but herself.
